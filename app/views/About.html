About Me

Technology has always been a passion of mine, which inspired me to take up Computer Science for my undergraduate studies. During my studies at Kurukshetra University I started exploring various aspects of computer science. The area which interested me most was front end development. I learnt basics of HTML, CSS and JavaScript and started developing my own websites. This helped me get a full time position at OpenFirms consulting. My main responsibility was to develop client's website using HTML, CSS, JS and CMS like Joomla and Magento. I also learnt some backend programming using PHP.

My short stint with PHP at OpenFirms left me wanting to learn more about backend programming. With goal of learning backend programming, I joined University of California, Santa Cruz, Silicon Valley Extension. I took various courses to hone my backend skills in Java and Python. I also took few courses in Android programming and have developed a full fledged app "Hyper Garage Sale". In order to apply what I learn at UCSCx, I joined moBack Inc. as Software Developer Intern. I have worked on various projects at moBack exploring full stack of web development.

Working on the projects at moBack Inc. and OpenFirms I have realized that the function of an engineer is to explore and understand the constantly changing aspects of technology and requirements of customers. And to learn to design and develop complex subsystems when necessary, but at the same time keep the solution simple and intuitive to the end user/customer. My internal sense of exploring and tinkering is perfect for the fast pace of the software world. I am always ready to learn new things while being able to apply what I know to the new. I love going to work and taking on any challenge that may come.
